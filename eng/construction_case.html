What we did

Taeyang C&T boasts innovative technology and delicate completion.

We were selected as an official company that can repair interiors and facilities by Korea National Housing Corporation in 2001.
From that time to 2010, we signed a long-term construction contract with them, constructing national housing facilities
and common areas for about 10 years.
We pride ourselves on having a wealth of experience and know-how accumulated in the repair of collective facilities such as apartments.
