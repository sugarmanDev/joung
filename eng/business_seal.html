Water Proof Systems

The world's first 100% waterproof construction.

This is a new concept waterproofing material that restores damage to the waterproofing layer with excellent adhesion and flexibility, preventing water leakage semi-permanently.

method guide

Polias method
Turbo Seal GT + Turbo Sheet / New Technology No. 376 designated by the Ministry of Construction and Transportation
Turbo Seal H + Turbo Sheet / Excellent Procurement Product
The Polyas method is a new concept waterproofing method
that can shorten the construction time and build a complete waterproof system
with a combination waterproofing method of turbo seal and turbo sheet.
Combined wall waterproofing
DW sheet + Turbo seal D + DW mesh
This is a waterproofing method that secures adhesion by
integrating the waterproof layer and concrete by first making a waterproof layer
on a building or civil structure using DW sheet
and DW mesh for a high-adhesion flexible type turbo seal.
Root barrier/waterproof for rooftop greening
Eco Seal + Eco Root barrier Sheet / New Technology No. 506 designated by the Ministry of Land, Transport and Maritime Affairs
This is a new concept waterproof and root barrier method
that can solve both waterproofing and root penetration
by attaching an Eco root barrier sheet after coating an adhesive,
flexible waterproof material on the base surface.
Method of reforming the waterproof layer
Leakage repair / ISO TR16475 standard established,
Designated as New Technology No. 376 by the Ministry of Construction and Transportation / New Disaster Prevention Technology No. 44-1
This targets the case where the existing waterproofing layer is damaged
or deteriorated, or the structure without waterproofing layer.
This is a method of fundamentally blocking water leakage by re-forming
the waterproof layer by injecting an adhesive flexible
Turbo Seal after drilling the existing waterproof layer or soil layer (or rock layer).
Turbo-Sheet GTR
Designated as New Technology No. 740 by the Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport

Turbo-Sheet GTR


As a self-adhesive sheet that integrates eco-friendly high-adhesion recycled rubber and membrane sheet,
it is a waterproof material that adheres to the concrete surface
with the stickiness of adhesive gel without applying primer or heat.

Pre-GTR(Top-down waterproof sheet)


This is a composite sheet that integrates a water-reactive
polymer coating material and a membrane sheet to which the top-down method can be applied.

GTR GEL / GTR GEL S


This is a waterproofing material that can be integrated and attached
to concrete concrete to be placed, and is mainly used for reinforcement of vulnerable parts
of leakage such as eco-friendly sewage bends and piles.
